7 Days in Bali --- 巴厘7日游

　　This is a travel log of my family’trip to Bali in Y2007.

Day0: The family took the 2-hour fligh to Bali from Singapore and arrived at about 8pm on 16/Jan.

  

Day1: Found a travel agent inside the hotel to make our travel plans. And booked a rafting trip on 18/Jan, elephant riding on 19/Jan and a shopping/city-viewing trip on 17/Jan.  Total cost US$330.  Then set out for the city-viewing trip to Ubud. The travel guide seemed to try to get us to all the outrageously expensive tourist shops until we told him to avoid these places otherwise we would look for another guide. Then he took us to a store called Galluh, price of which was more reasonable, with 20% discount. (Later another driver told us he could get 50% discount in the same store!) We had lunch at a road-side Babi Gulling store. The food tasted great but the store environment was just terrible. Anyway I absolutely loved it though the grandpa/ma may not agree with me. Had a rest back at the hotel before we went to the Ulawatu sunset view and Jimbaran seafood dinner.The food in the Jimbaran store was just so so, but the view and atmosphere offered was absolutely marvelous. This has to be one of the best place in the world to have a romantic dinner. 脚下海浪轻拂，远方红日西下，海鲜美酒，世人皆醉

  

 

Day2: White water rafting. There are many companies operating rafting activities in Bali. And this was the 2nd time we went for rafting in Bali and I think we have had a better deals in Telaga Waja river. The rafting trip was fantastic and everybody loved it. Dinner at the famous TELAGA NAGA Chinese restaurant operated by Bali Hyatt in Sanur. It has a beautiful garden setting with ponds and ducks, bridges, pavilions, and usually well prepared Chinese food. This is definitely a place to be if you do not mind the overseas Chinese cooking.

  

 

Day3: Elephant trekking. This time I am not sure if we got the best deal for elephant trekking. Anyway, the place we went (forgot the name) is the big billboard of elephant riding scattered just about every corner of the island. Rest assured, we had great fun. ANd we also leared that Bali actually does not produce elephant and all the 10 elephants in the park are imported from Sumatra, including the elephant trainers. Note: make sure select a male elephant, with ivory. And a little tip could produce magic. Dinner at a local restraunt in Sanur with Balinese Frog Dance. Great food, great entainment.

   

Day4: Snorkeling. Aha, This is a disappointment. We went to a water sports center located in Nusa Dua and did not have much fun, primiarily because one member of the group had seasick again. But the evening shopping trip in Kuta was certainly one of the highlights. Great stuff, great bargain. A Bali t-shirt can go as low as RP20k. Hint: remember to bargain starting at least 20% of the asking price. Had the best food in a seafood restaurant (Mini Seafood) in Kuta. Crab is a must try.

  

 

Day5: Photo studio. This is the hightligh of the whole trip, a best kept secret of Bali: taking family photo in Balinese dress in a traditional Balinese house by the best photographer. The session took almost 3 hours and produced nearly 250 photos, from which we needed to select 60 to make into albums. Price: US$400. Let me know if you are interested to know more. In the afternoon the grandpas went for a 2-hour spa session in the hotel and the rest of us went to Kuta shopping again. Dinner at friend’s wedding banquet.

  

Day6: Home again. Woke up at 6:10am. Same day I arrived in Kuantan again. Back to reality.

Conclusion: overall great experience.

Recommendation: white water rafting, dinner in all 4 places mentioned (especially Jimbaran), Kuta shopping, buying some t-shirts in Bali Prelimilary Design store near Sanur market (owner claimed he made the designs himself), studio photo.

Infomation: driver (7-seater SUV) Gonji (+62 8123607829, quite a humble yet smart guy. Never told you how much he would charge, instead asking you to name any amount of money to pay, which made you willing to give him more. Generally RP22K for a 5h shopping trip or RP35K for whole day.

Thank you.
